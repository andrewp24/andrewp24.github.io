Association for Education and Health in Central America

Privacy Policy

Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. respects and protects your privacy. Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. will never sell or rent your personal information to other individuals or companies. When you make a donation, your name will be included in Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. newsletters and other publications. If you do not wish to share your name, or you choose to make your donation anonymously, Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. will not publish your name.

When you create an account with Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. you will receive an acknowledgment of your donation for your records, and occasional email and postal delivery updates. If you do not wish to hear from us, simply uncheck the box on the bottom of the billing page, unsubscribe from any mailing or contact us directly.

  • LINKS

    This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site.

  • REFERRALS

    If you refer or inform a friend about our site, you will be asked for the friend’s name and email address. Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. will send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email and will not add your friend to our mailing list.

  • GIFT DONATIONS

    If you make a donation as a gift on Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc.’s website, we will keep the gift recipient’s information confidential in the same way that we keep your information confidential.

  • CHANGES TO THE POLICY

    Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc. periodically reviews and updates our policy for communicating with you via occasional email updates. We will post any updates about that policy on our website.
